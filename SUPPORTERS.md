# KTP Supporters

KTP (Keep the Practice) runs on donated time and donated money. These are the
people who chip in every month to keep the game servers, the stats backend, and
the anti-cheat online. Thank you, genuinely.

**Want to join them?** [Set up a small monthly sponsorship](https://github.com/sponsors/afraznein).
Even a few bucks a month covers a real slice of the hosting bill.

> **Recognition only.** Being listed here is a thank-you and nothing more.
> Sponsorship never affects a match, a queue, roster spots, or anything
> competitive. Keeping the money and the games completely separate is the whole
> point of a league that runs an anti-cheat.
