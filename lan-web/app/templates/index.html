We're convening companies of Day of Defeat operators on the basement floor of the TAP Esport Center, Philadelphia — the same Chinatown room that hosted the 2025 LAN. The last day of July through the first weekend of August, 2026. Whether you're old guard, new blood, or somewhere bewildered in between, this is your dossier. Read it. Sign it. Bring it with you.

Friday opens with the draft event — a standalone pickup tournament open to anyone in attendance who wants in, seats limited at the door. It runs independently of the weekend's team competition. Saturday is the group stage: every company plays a fixed slate to settle seeding. Sunday is the playoffs — bracket to a verdict, finals Sunday evening.

Inside this dossier you'll find your order of battle (who's registered and with whom), your communiqué (the Saturday schedule), the bracket (Sunday's road to the title), and your rules of engagement (what's a fair shot and what's a forfeit). Captains submit seeding ballots once rosters are locked.

— Get ready for a weekend of fun, sweat, and dod innit.
